About Aureum

Aurium (AUR) is designed as a scalable, community-driven token on the **BNB Smart Chain (BSC)**, aimed at powering a diverse Web3 ecosystem: Play-to-Earn games, NFTs with utility and DeFi integrations. The protocol emphasizes sustainability, transparency and long-term value creation for holders.

  • ✅ Built on **BSC** for low fees and high transaction throughput.
  • ✅ Tokenomics that reward long-term holders and fund ecosystem growth.
  • ✅ Governance planned via multisig & timelock for critical changes.

Why BNB Smart Chain?

Ultra-low transaction costs and wide DeFi ecosystem make the BSC ideal for game and NFT interactions, enabling Aurium to scale user experiences with minimal friction and maximum speed.
