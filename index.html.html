About Aureum

         

Aurium (AUR) is designed as a scalable, community-driven token on Polygon, aimed at powering a diverse Web3 ecosystem: Play-to-Earn games, NFTs with utility and DeFi integrations. The protocol emphasizes sustainability, transparency and long-term value creation for holders.

         
               
  • • Built on Polygon for low fees and high throughput.
    •            
  • • Tokenomics that reward long-term holders and fund growth.
    •            
  • • Governance planned via multisig & timelock for critical changes.
    •          
       
       
         

Why Polygon?

         

Low transaction costs and wide DeFi ecosystem make Polygon ideal for game and NFT interactions — enabling Aurium to scale user experiences with minimal friction.

       
     
   
 
   
   

Key Benefits

   
     
       

Rewards for Holders

       

2% of every transaction is redistributed automatically among holders — no staking required.

       
     
       

Sustainable Development Fund

       

A portion of fees funds marketing, partnerships and development of games & NFT utilities.

     
     
       

Planned Liquidity Reinforcement

       

Liquidity increases are executed manually every quarter on the AUR/USDC pair to improve market depth.

     
   
 
   
   
     

Tokenomics

     

Fees differ depending on transaction direction. All fees are transparent and governed via on-chain events and multisig operations.
